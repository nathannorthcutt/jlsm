## Summary

Optimization decisions for partitioned tables: hotspot mitigation for
sequential inserts, compaction strategies that account for partition
boundaries, and partition pruning for vector similarity queries. All three
were deferred pending WD-07 (Partitioning & Rebalancing) — now specified.

Decisions in this cluster: sequential-insert-hotspot,
partition-aware-compaction, vector-query-partition-pruning

- Full features: sequential-insert-hotspot, partition-aware-compaction
