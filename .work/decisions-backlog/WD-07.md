## Summary

Distributed data placement — partition ownership, replication, rebalancing,
and compaction for multi-node operation. Safety-critical items
(un-walled-memtable-data-loss, in-flight-write-protection) should be promoted
as soon as rebalancing work begins. Replication and rebalancing require
transport + membership from Cluster 5.
