## Summary

During compaction, re-encrypt blocks under a new DEK (for DEK rotation/retirement). Maintains correctness: in-flight reads see consistent DEK even during rewrite.

## Acceptance Criteria

- encryption.primitives-lifecycle compaction-migration requirements direct-annotated
- Test: concurrent read during compaction-rewrite returns correct plaintext
- Test: rollback on failed compaction does not partially migrate blocks
